Odoo WooCommerce Connector

With this fastest-growing digital transformation of business, many powerful eCommerce platforms available in business today, use of WooCommerce plugin provided by WordPress for their B2B, B2C selling is most in demand as it allows Business owners to create an online store and sell most conveniently with many built-in features.

To manage various functionalities that the WooCommerce plugin provides as a powerful e-Commerce platform, the need for the most effective ERP management System that operates all the functionalities providing the most user-friendly environment is a must. Odoo is one such best and most famous Open source ERP management systems providing excellent, all-in-one features the use of it for all backend operations lets your business reach new heights providing multi scheme architecture in one platform, and hence use of Odoo wooCommerce integrator becomes vital for you to implement in your business.

Odoo WooCommerce Integrator will help you manage all Operations of your WooCommerce store in Odoo by integrating the WooCommerce plugin with odoo ERP. This connector allows to perform various functionalities like Importing Customerâ€™s Data, Orders, Products and their Categories, Product Tags, Stock Details, Reports, Invoices, Returns, Refunds, Exporting and Updating Products tags, Product Categories, Coupon Details, and Products Stock. It also allows automation of various vital tasks like Importing Orders, their Status Update, and Stock Update functionalities to ease Users' work and increase potential outcomes in business.

    Advantages

    Connect Multiple WooCommerce Stores

    Allows to connect multiple WooCommerce stores with a single Odoo database and thereby facilitates to Manage, Operate, and Process of the Data from a centralized Platform.

    Automated Workflow

    Various operations like Confirming Order, Creating and Validating Invoices, and Register Payment can be operated in a fully automated way.

    Synchronize Products

    Synchronizes the functions of Import, Export, and Update products and their details between WooCommerce and Odoo.

    Financial Status of WooCommerce Orders

    Allows you to take necessary actions like Confirming orders, Creating and Validating Invoices, and many such functionalities of all Financial Status of Orders in WooCommerce directly from Odoo.

    Schedule base Synchronization

    Allows various Processes of Import, Export, and Update to be operated in an Automated Way during Scheduled Based Time decided by the User.

    Real-Time Synchronization via Webhook

    Allows various processes of Import like Import Products, Orders, and Customers to be operated in an Automated Way at any instant of time.

    Process History

    Helps to Analyze the failures in any of the Operations performed whether it is of Import Process, Export Process, or Update Process.

    Cancel and Refunds Management

    Manage details of Cancel and Refunds of Orders between WooCommerce and Odoo

    Stock Management

    Manage details of Stock between WooCommerce and Odoo

    Price Management

    Synchronizes the details about Changes in the Price of Products between WooCommerce and Odoo.
